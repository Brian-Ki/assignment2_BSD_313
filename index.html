Kobe Bean Bryant

1978 – 2020 | The Black Mamba

The Legacy

Kobe Bryant was more than just an extraordinary basketball player; he was a global icon whose relentless work ethic, famously known as the "Mamba Mentality," inspired millions around the world to pursue excellence in all facets of life.

Career Highlights

  • 5× NBA Champion (2000–2002, 2009, 2010)
  • 2× NBA Finals MVP (2009, 2010)
  • 18× NBA All-Star
  • 2× Olympic Gold Medalist (2008, 2012)
  • Academy Award winner for Best Animated Short Film (Dear Basketball, 2018)
"The most important thing is to try and inspire people so that they can be great in whatever they want to do." — Kobe Bryant
